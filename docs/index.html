Air quality in Frome

Tech Shed Frome in partnership with Frome Town Council are developing a system around low-cost air quality sensors that are placed in key sites around Frome.

We are listing our Frome sensors on the luftdaten project, an international community air quality initiative.

What do you mean by air quaility?

We measure air quality by the capturing level of particulate matter. This is produced principally by vehicle exhaust emissions, dust from vehicle brake linings and burning wood. The particulate matter is measured in 2/.5 and 10.0 Micron sizes, and in quantities of parts per million in the air, we breathe. Empowering the community with information

Adverse health effects caused by air pollution are increasingly being recognised and debated at a national and international level. Our aim is to empower the community with information on how vehicle traffic and other sources of pollutants can impact air quality over time.
